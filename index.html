Progression Plan

✅ Weeks 1–2 · Foundation (15–20 min)

2 sets per exercise · Light weight · Perfect form · Build the habit and perfect your form.

Weeks 3–4 · Build (20–30 min)

3 sets · Add 2–3 reps · Reduce rest by 10s · Keep pushing — you're building real strength.

Weeks 5–6 · Load (30–35 min)

3–4 sets · Add 2.5–5 lbs on main lifts · Full program sessions.

Weeks 7–8 · Advance (35–45 min)

Resistance bands · Consider 4th training day · Track 1RM progress.

Every 8 Weeks · Deload

2 sets, 50% weight · Full recovery week · Come back stronger.
